What is a password generator?

A password generator is a tool that automatically creates a password based on guidelines you define to provide strong and unpredictable passwords for each of your accounts.

What characterizes a strong password?

Long: The longer a password is, the more secure it is. A strong password must be at least 12 characters long.

Random: Strong passwords use a combination of letters, numbers, capital letters, and symbols to form an unpredictable string of characters that don't resemble words or names.

Unique: A strong password should be unique for each account to reduce your vulnerability in the event of a hack.
